Roller Coaster Simulations

Roller coaster simulations are applications that RCEs* use to design a roller coaster and they show the forces that would be put on the rider, and or show the first person view of the Roller coaster.

NoLimits 2 NoLimits 2 is a roller coaster simulation software that allows users to create and experience realistic roller coaster designs. It features advanced physics, detailed graphics, and a wide range of customization options for designing coasters and parks.
