Skyrush

Skyrush is a steel hyper coaster, that is 200 ft tall and has a drop of 205 ft.
This roller coaster is an Intamin Wing coaster, it has normal seating with somewhere for your feet to rest in the middle 2 seats,
But on the outer 2 seats your feet dangle, those seats are considered "wing seats".
this roller coaster has no inversions, Max G-force of 5 which means that the rider feels like they are pushed towards the earth at 5x earths gravity
