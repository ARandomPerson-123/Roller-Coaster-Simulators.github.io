Fahrenheit

Photo by Jrosenberry( User Doesn't exsit) on Wikimedia using the CC BY SA license

Fahrenheit is a ride at hershey park that has a beyond vertical drop.
It held the record of steepest drop with other roller coasters.
Fahrenheit is 121 ft tall, has 2,700 ft of track, 6 inversion (The most at hersheypark),
a Max speed of 58 MPH and takes 1 minute and 25 to go through the entire track and most importantly
a 97 degree drop hence the name fahrenheit!
