Storm Runner

Storm runner is a hydraulically launched coaster that goes over a 150 ft tall top hat before going down a drop of 180 ft.
Storm runner was the first hydraulic launch coaster to invert. This roller coaster has 3 inversions a ridetime of 50 seconds after the break before the launch
it also was the first roller coaster to have a dual-loading station with switch tracks, it also was the first accelerator to feature Over The Shoulder Restraints.
Storm runner goes from 0-72 MPH in 2 seconds in the launch.
