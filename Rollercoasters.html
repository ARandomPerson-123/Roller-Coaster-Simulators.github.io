Roller coasters

Roller coasters are a type of amusement ride that is designed to be thrilling. Roller coasters come in many different types which include: inverted, which you sit below the track; inverting, which riders go upside down; launching, which uses a LIM*, LSM**, or Hydraulic Launch to propell the train full of riders.
